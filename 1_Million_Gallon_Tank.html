Cathodic Protection Installation for a 1 Million Gallon Water Storage Tank

Two views of impressed current anodes installed above the bottom of the tank in PVC holders to prevent contact with the tank bottom.

The header cable to the anodes was run up through the vent to the outside.

The header cable from the interior anodes was run across the top of the tank and down the overflow pipe in conduit strapped to the pipe.
